Robert Shippey
Computer Systems (Networks)



Member Bio: Robert Shippey is a 3rd year Computer Systems student at NTU. He achieved a First in his Level 1 and Level 2 results. In his second year he was selected to be a Student Mentor to first years studying Computer Systems, this involved running 'drop in' surgery session where students could get advice about classwork, coursework, or any worries about University life in general. As part of being a Mentor, he was given the opportunity to assist in first year C++ labs. From his course, he has learnt many programming languages, project management techniques and methodologies, networking fundamentals, and specific server technologies. Beyond university, Robert is interested in Web design/development as he believes that the web is the platform where all new development will happen. More information about his project (both Uni and personal) can be found at his personal website.
