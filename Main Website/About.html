About us

Welcome to the NTU Computing Society website! We are an official founded society that allow for all people from with interests in computing at Nottingham Trent University to meet up, have social events and also provide help with anyone who may be struggling with their learning.

We aren't all about going out and partying. Our main goals and objectives are to provide a friendly group of like minded individuals in which people from the computing courses at NTU will feel comfortable to hang out with. We have other social events in mind; like paint balling, gaming / movie nights and revision sessions for the looming exams! We also have some interesting new developments for our society, we are constantly expanding.

We all hope that creating this society will make each and all of your university experiences even easier and more enjoyable.

We'll be making a big entrance at freshers fair 2012 our stalls can be seen at

Number 14 at city and number 54 at clifton see the student union's fair for maps

Our current President is Thomas Knowles, and vice Jacob Gould

We maintain an active committee for members to get involved in joining our vibrant committee in making key decisions and taking part in our exciting expanding society.
